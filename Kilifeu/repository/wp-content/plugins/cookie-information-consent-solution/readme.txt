== Description ==
[youtube https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DbhGtz6BJgw]

This GDPR & ePrivacy cookie consent solution plugin will easily implement Cookie Information's Consent Solution onto your website.

The plugin will add the necessary scripts and SDK libary to the WordPress installation.

The solution contains:
* Consent pop-up
* Privacy Controls – opt-in and opt-out buttons
* Cookie Policy based on scan of your website
* Cookie control SDK – blocking of cookies
* Legally required consent log

It takes advantage of the WPML plugin for WordPress to automatically change the language desired for the Consent Solution.

In order to use the features and funtions of this plugin, it is necessary to register your domain/site with Cookie Information.

Register here: [Register here](https://cookieinformation.com/registration)

== Installation ==
How to install:

1. Upload (cookie-information-consent-solution.zip)  through the \"Plugins\" menu in Wordpress admin
2. ACTIVATE the plugin
3. Copy/paste the shortcodes provided in the Cookie information menu tab to the referred page on your site..

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Is the solution FREE? =
Our solution is based on a 30 day completely FREE trial period with no credit card needed. Once the trial has ended the scanner will no longer crawl your website(s).
To see our prices, go to: [https://cookieinformation.com/prices](https://cookieinformation.com/prices)
= Do I need to register my website? =
Yes, in order for this plugin to work and you getting the full functionalities of our product, you need to register your domain at: [Register here](https://cookieinformation.com/registration).
Our servers need to know which domains to look at for our scanner to crawl you website and provide you with a cookiepolicy.
= Can I edit my consent solution =
YES! When you register you get access to our platform where you'll have complete control over your consent solution.
= Can I style my consent popup =
You will have access to a variety of templates to choose from, which can be customized to your preference. See template examples here: [Design Center](https://templates.cookieinformation.com/)
= How often will your website get scanned =
The scanning frequency can be set to either monthly or daily. The choice is yours.
